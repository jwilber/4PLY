7PLY MAGAZINE

7PLY is an independent magazine taking a data-driven approach to skateboarding content.

7PLY was created to fill a void currently existing in skate media content, with two goals in mind. First, to provide content that skateboarders find interesting. Second, to provide skateboarding-related content with a quantitative spin. Think of some combination between Jenkem Mag and FiveThirtyEight.

Above all, we aim to produce engaging content. If you have any ideas, datasets, or pitches you'd like to discuss, we'd love to hear from you.
