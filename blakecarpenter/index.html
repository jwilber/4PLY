What do you think your top tricks are?

Top Tricks

Sick to see tricks like switch flip back tail and switch tre lip so high.
I just dont want to do too many of one trick but at the same time it’s like you might as well do what you can do. Like on a trip if I go out with a new group or something im like well this is a good trick and they've probably never seen this before so fuck it.

If youre on a trip is it more about getting everyone stoked and having a good time or is it more of like a mission?
If I’m going to a city where I know for a fact something’s there and I can skate it, I'm on vendetta mode. I'm like ‘Let's hit that spot I need this trick to be in this shit.’

Are you working on anything at the moment? What was up with the Houston trip?
I'm on my own filming this Nike Blazer video part. It's gonna be like a little video with Grant Taylor as the star obviously and then everyone just has footage in blazers.

Love blazers.
They're the best. They're so good. Yeah I started like 5 months ago and I've just been like getting a filmer if they're coming to town I’ll like race to Miami and skate down there. It's been going good.

I know you’ve mentioned before you don’t end up filming a lot of lines on trips because it takes so much of the filmers time. But someone wanted to know if you 'physically don’t know how to string a line together'.
Yeah I feel like selfish when I go film a line. Especially when it's not as fun. Sometimes it is what it is. Like if I just have my own guy ready whos like lets go to this line spot im like fuck yeah lets do it you know.

So for this Nike thing and you’ve got your own filmer are you filming more lines?
He doesn't have a lens ahaha. It's unfortunate but whatever, I just make the best with what I got.

How did your part in Daylando come about?
It was a little determination when I was like 15 and 16 and then I like lost it because I didn't think it was going to be a reality to be a pro skater. Then TJ Gaskill basically forced me to film the rest of my part and we did it. I think I was slacking and he just fuckin made sure that we got some good shit and that was it.

Shoutout TJ!
Yup! Shoutout TJ, saved each other's lives.

Do you think your skating has changed much since then?
I’d say like, the things that I'm capable of doing, like athletically, I feel like I can do more now. I've been going to some of the shit that I skated back then and I'm like, 'Sick it got easier to skate this shit right now'.




BLAKE OVER TIME


When I look at these charts I always think about how like Jerry Hsu’s Bag of Suck part is mostly switch and nollie because of an ankle injury. You had a big dip in your switch skating around 2017, did something happen?
I think so yeah, like actually swear to god. At the end of Chronicles one of the last tricks I wanted to try was switch bigflip front board before I could do them really. It's like this rail that Sean Malto back over crook nollie flipped. It's like a long rail and I just like drove 2 hours straight to it and I went to warm up with like a switch board and on the switch board I just like folded my shit. I just sat right on it on my left ankle. So yeah like deadass that happened. I fucked myself and I never even got it looked at really. That was before I was like down to go get shit checked out and like worked on. And I just worked it myself and it has full range again its fine.

You take physical therapy more seriously now?
It's real! I’m 28 and about to be 29 and I definitely understand how special it is to do it now. I’m just lookin at Tony Hawk dude, 52 years old and he won’t stop until he dies I don't think.

You were saying athletically you feel like you’re more capable now?
Honestly. I get more sore but like I know how to skate better because I've been skating longer ahaha. It makes sense. Like I started skating when I was 12. Some people say thats like late but.. 🤷

Whats good with all the bump to bars?
I just like skating them, testing the limits. I always wanted to ollie high.

You’re not much of a tranny guy, you think its the best way for street folk to get that air time?
Yeah forreal, just testing the wings. I grew up at this park with minimal tranny and never got my ramp legs. Been working on it, tranny part coming when I’m 35.



So how do you the Pyramid Country guys? You have so many clips in Arizona.
I got in the car with Grant one day to just go on a trip and we were driving cross country and I ended up in Arizona and we met up with those guys and it happened to be the Boyish premiere on that weekend. And then I just like met all of them and I was just like ‘these dudes are the sickest, I wanna hang out with them’. Then they let me hang out with them and that was it. I followed them before that premiere and I was like ‘Dude we're gonna be there for that? Holy shit.’

What was good with Asphalt Yacht Club
Basically they started with so much money, Stevie and Stefan were the co owners along with a bunch of investor millionaire guys. It was going so good and all the money was flowing into it and they were making clothes but they weren’t that sick and everyone was wearing them, we were all doing it. We were going on trips and it was fucking tight. It was like a good company to ride for, riley and figgy and nyjah. Nyjah made it so big our demos were like fucked. Then one day I just got an offer from Nike and I just went to Nike apparel.

Is AYC still around?
We were just talking about it the other day and we were looking on instagram trying to check. They don't have an instagram anymore ahaha. They were funny though. We had so much fun on those trips.

Any yachts?
Dude we were staying at this mansion in Miami and Stevie just like pulled up a yacht on the dock and we went out and he had strippers and shit on it and we were just like partying on the yacht. We had to uphold the name.

How could you not?
Exactly.

How did you end up on King of the Road? You were flow then right?
I think they just had a stint where some people were hurt and somebody didn’t want to go on KOTR and they were like let's bring the hungry flow guys.

Is it more fun or hellish?
Its fun for like 30 minutes of the day when you roll up to a spot and you’re getting to like hypnotize chickens and shit but besides that it fuckin sucks. I was getting to the point where I would just get made and just be like I’m sleeping in like sorry, til 10. And mike would be like dude we’re all waiting at the van or whatever and im just like well we cant leave til we’re ready to skate so fuck, sorry’ ahaha

What's the worst part?
Dude just like exhaustion, pure exhaustion. Two weeks straight of bullshitting and trying to hit everything you possibly can. Dude thats like why I didn't skate much on those things cause like I didn't want to get hurt on some stupid shit. Your hips are like all closed up, you’re sitting in the van like a pile of shit drinking all day its weird.

Sounds rough.
Yeah it was an experience. I did it twice too. It was fucked. The second time they micd us up and like had us act like we were on a fuckin reality tv show or something.

The vice one?
Yeah cause vice had like an actual production crew for every team and they had like bits that they would like almost throw out at you at some points you know. Theyd be like oh uhh this and thats happening over there, were just like what? You’re trying to make this like a show right now its insane. I mean it was a show. We didnt get paid enough for that shit. That shit aired for so long.

Did it feel more like a reality show than a skate trip?
A lot of the times. Like they have to have you liked pulled aside doing an interview about something that just happened or something that will happen. Yeah idk. Im glad I dont gotta go back. I think it's canceled forever.
